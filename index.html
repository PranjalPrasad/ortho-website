About Us

Pune's Most Trusted Orthopaedic Specialists

At OrthoCare, we combine decades of surgical expertise with the latest technology to deliver outcomes that restore mobility, reduce pain, and transform lives. Our fellowship-trained surgeons, physiotherapists, and specialists provide holistic musculoskeletal care.

Founded in 2006, we've grown from one clinic on FC Road to four branches across Pune, serving 15,000+ patients annually. NABH-accredited and ISO-certified.

What We Treat

Our Core Orthopaedic Services

World-class surgical and non-surgical care for every orthopaedic condition

Joint Replacement

Total & partial hip, knee & shoulder replacement with advanced implants for lasting mobility.

Spine Surgery

Minimally invasive procedures for disc herniation, scoliosis & degenerative spine conditions.

Sports Medicine

Expert management of ligament tears, muscle strains, cartilage damage & overuse injuries.

Fracture Care

Emergency and elective fracture treatment with modern fixation for optimal recovery.

Arthroscopy

Keyhole surgical procedures for knee, shoulder & hip with minimal scarring & faster recovery.

Physiotherapy

Customised rehabilitation programs by certified physiotherapists for post-surgery & injury recovery.
